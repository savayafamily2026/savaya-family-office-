## Section 02 — Investment Process

- Eyebrow: 02 Investment Process
- Headline: Investment Process.
- Intro: Capital moves through six deliberate stages — and proceeds re-enter at the top. Permanent by structure, not by timeline.
- Center label: Permanent Capital

1. Origination — Proprietary sourcing across our sectors and relationships.
2. Diligence — Technical, commercial, and legal assessment in depth.
3. Decisioning — Investment Committee review against a written policy.
4. Execution — Structuring and deployment of long-term capital.
5. Monitoring — Active oversight, governance, and value creation.
6. Realization — Exit or recycle — proceeds returned to the family.
