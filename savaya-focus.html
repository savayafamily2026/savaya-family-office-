A structured three-tier liquidity architecture.

SAVAYA maintains a disciplined tiered liquidity framework ensuring the family's operational and strategic needs are met at all times — irrespective of market conditions or portfolio positioning.

I
Immediate Access · < 30 Days
Operational Reserve
Cash, money market, short-term treasuries. Sized to cover 12–18 months of family operating expenses.
II
Tactical Access · 30 – 180 Days
Tactical Liquidity Pool
Public equities, liquid alternatives, listed credit. Fund opportunistic deployment or bridge private commitments.
III
Strategic Lock-up · 1 – 10+ Years
Core Illiquid Portfolio
Private equity, real assets, infrastructure, direct operating stakes. Long-duration, return-seeking capital.

Risk Management Principles

Concentration LimitsNo single investment exceeds 15% of total portfolio at cost. Sector allocations are bounded by IPS policy ranges reviewed annually.
Currency DiversificationPortfolio maintained across USD, AED, EUR, and select EM exposures. No single currency exceeds 50% of financial assets.
Quarterly Stress TestingScenario analysis against: global recession, Gulf regional disruption, major currency dislocation, and liquidity crisis.
Independent OversightInvestments above defined thresholds require Investment Committee review. The family principal retains ultimate authority.

Every position is held through a structural lens, not a cyclical one.

SAVAYA does not trade themes. When we identify a structural transformation, we commit capital for a decade or more — building concentrated, high-conviction positions where we have informational or relational edge.
