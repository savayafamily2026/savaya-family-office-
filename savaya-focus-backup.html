01Focus Areas

Five structural transformations, held for a decade or more.

02Liquidity Architecture

A structured three-tier liquidity architecture.

A disciplined tiered framework ensures the family's operational and strategic needs are met at all times — irrespective of market conditions or portfolio positioning.

I
Immediate Access · < 30 Days

Operational Reserve

Cash, money market, and short-term treasuries. Sized to cover 12–18 months of family operating expenses.

II
Tactical Access · 30 – 180 Days

Tactical Liquidity Pool

Public equities, liquid alternatives, and listed credit. Funds opportunistic deployment or bridges private commitments.

III
Strategic Lock-up · 1 – 10+ Years

Core Illiquid Portfolio

Private equity, real assets, infrastructure, and direct operating stakes. Long-duration, return-seeking capital.

03Risk Management Principles

01 Concentration Limits

No single investment exceeds 15% of total portfolio at cost. Sector allocations are bounded by IPS policy ranges reviewed annually.

02 Currency Diversification

Portfolio maintained across USD, AED, EUR, and select EM exposures. No single currency exceeds 50% of financial assets.

03 Quarterly Stress Testing

Scenario analysis against global recession, Gulf regional disruption, major currency dislocation, and liquidity crisis.

04 Independent Oversight

Investments above defined thresholds require Investment Committee review. The family principal retains ultimate authority.

Philosophy

Every position is held through a structural lens, not a cyclical one.

We do not trade themes. When we identify a structural transformation, we commit capital for a decade or more — building concentrated, high-conviction positions where we hold informational or relational edge.
